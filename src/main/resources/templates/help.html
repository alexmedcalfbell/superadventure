Super Adventure is a natural language (illustrated) text adventure. That means you can move around and interact with a scene in a fairly 'natural' way. Commands are interpreted based on various predefined keywords, some of which are related to movement and others that link actions to targets.

  • To move between locations, you might say: Go to the north
  • To interact with something in a location you could say: Talk to the witch
  • Though you could equally have said: Talk witch or Talk to the evil looking witch over there
In the response window you will see certain words highlighted. These are keywords that you can use as hints on how to interact with the scene. For example:
You stand before a vast lake. An ugly old man stands near the edge of the water . To the South lies the overgrown path .
